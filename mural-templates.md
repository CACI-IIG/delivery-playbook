## Mural Templates

Mural is an amazing tool for facilitating any sort of conversation. This could be your team's regular retrospective, collaborative surveys, tools, or visual models to give people a new lens on a challenge. 

All the following Mural templates are ones that have been used by our teams and can either be used out the box or provide inspiration for creating your own. 

A warning that some do contain copywrite images, and not all of them contain explicit instructions on how they should be run. If you want any support or have suggestions do reach out and we will look to help and improve them. This is also a public repository, so please contribute your own examples to help the community.
