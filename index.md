# delivery-playbook
An open collection of delivery playbooks, practical guidance, reusable templates, and proven delivery patterns.

## Content

* [Manual of Me for Teams](manual-of-me-for-teams.md) - An expanded template and session format to use manuals of me within your team.
* [Mural Templates](mural-templates.md) - Readily available Mural whiteboards to help you run sessions  

## Intent
We want to create an archive of approaches, ideas, tools, and playbooks that help everyone achieve their delivery goals. We spend a lot of our time taking what we find on the internet, trying it out, adapting it, and learning what works in our own environments. This learning is the juice we want to share amongst the delivery, engineering, agile, change, business... everybody community. There is no best practice, only lived experience we can share and adapt into our next context.
